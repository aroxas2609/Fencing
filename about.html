About Johnstone Fencing

Straight answers, solid fences, and service that respects your time — with a speciality in chainlink fencing in Sydney for sport, rural, and public-sector sites.

Our story

Built on reliability & craft

Johnstone Fencing started with a simple goal: deliver fencing you can rely on — installed neatly, on schedule, and at a fair price. Today we are best known as chainlink fencing contractors for sports grounds, rural properties, and government or institutional sites across Sydney — though we still install Colorbond, timber, aluminium, and handle repairs when those are the better fit.

We focus on the details that matter on long runs: strainers, tension, gate hangings, and finishes that survive coastal humidity and inland heat. Whether it is kilometres of rural chainwire or a school oval in Sydney or the regions, we treat every job with the same care.

We understand council processes, site access for plant, and ground conditions across Greater Sydney, the Central Coast, Newcastle, and the Southern Highlands. You get clear communication from measure to handover — and we are easy to reach if you have questions after we leave.
